About Artful Barn

A one-of-a-kind creative space tucked in Springboro, Ohio — where art, nature, and community come together.

The Artful Barn — Springboro, Ohio
Our resident peacocks
Est. 2024 · Springboro, Ohio

Art should live
where life does

Artful Barn started with a simple idea: that beautiful, meaningful art should be accessible to everyone — not locked away in galleries that feel intimidating or far away. We wanted to create a space that felt like home.

Our converted barn sits on a property with a fenced peacock sanctuary, where the colours of folk art and the colours of nature feel completely in conversation with each other. Come for a class, a gallery visit, or just to see the peacocks.

Artful Barn is run by a team of women who believe art changes how we see our homes, our communities, and ourselves. Every piece we carry has a story worth knowing.
